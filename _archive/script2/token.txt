1. 🔐 Create a Personal Access Token
	1	Go to https://github.com/settings/tokens and generate a fine-grained PAT:
	◦	Scope: Allow access to jekyll-site-repo (content read/write).
	◦	Permissions:
	▪	Contents: Read and write
	▪	Metadata: Read
	2	Copy the token, and add it to vault-repo secrets as:
	◦	JEKYLL_REPO_PAT
